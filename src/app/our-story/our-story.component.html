Our Story

Once upon a time

The Bagelry, nestled in Bergen Park of Evergreen Colorado, originally opened in 1995 by a native New Yorker who missed the New York Style Bagel from back East. The grocery store "roll with a hole" was not cutting it. A bagel needs to be hand-crafted, boiled in water, and baked to perfection.

Baked fresh every morning, the mountain community of Evergreen embraced the fresh, doughy, and crispy alternative to frozen bagels and The Bagelry became a popular breakfast spot.

The Bagelry has continued to be a local hot spot for skiers, snowboarders, and commuters looking for a quick breakfast on their way out of town. It has cushy seating spaces to accommodate both families and groups, as well as those looking for a quiet space to work and use our free Wi-Fi. The ambiance includes murals and works from local artists. Local is a key factor in our motivation. We source out as many local ingredients and delicious products as possible. Only the best for our customers!

We would like to thank the Evergreen community for your business and support for the past twenty years. Thank you!
