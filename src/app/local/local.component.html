Locally Sourced

Part of The Bagelry's mission is to source as many local ingredients and delicious products as possible. Our bagels are proudly baked with Denver flour. We also offer 10 to 15 locally made products for our food and drink offerings. We live in a spectacular region that takes pride in producing products with beautiful, pure, and healthy ingredients. Using local helps us support our community, creates a sustainable food system, conserves energy by reducing transportation, and well just tastes better.
