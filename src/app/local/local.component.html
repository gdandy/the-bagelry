Locally Sourced

Part of The Bagelry's mission is to source as many local ingredients and delicious products as possible. We bake our bagels with local Denver flour and offer 10 to 15 locally made products for our food and drink offerings. We live in a spectacular region that takes pride in producing products with beautiful, pure, and healthy ingredients. Using local helps us support our community, creates a sustainable food system, conserves energy by reducing transportation, and well ii just tastes better. Here's some of our local offerings:

We proudly bake with local flour from Denver, CO. Sourcing local ingredients for our bagels is one of our top priorities! Only the best for our customers!

The Bagelry proudly serves coffee from Dazbog, a local Colorado company. All of their coffee is certified organic and roasted in small batches to insure quality and freshness. Come in and try our full menu of Espresso drinks or one of our four varieties of fresh brewed Dazbog coffee.
